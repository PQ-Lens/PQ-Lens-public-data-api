UM-MTE v1.0 was created as one research output of:

PQ-LENS - PQ Dashboard: Large Language Models for Enhanced Navigation,
Analysis and Synthesis of Maltese Parliamentary Questions

PQ-LENS is funded by XJENZA MALTA under the Digital Technologies Programme.
The University of Malta Principal Investigator is Prof. Joel Azzopardi.

UM-MTE is one dataset output of the wider PQ-LENS project. This release is not
the complete PQ-LENS project dataset and contains no Parliamentary Question
records.
